About

The work behind
the sound.

Bio. After graduating from Berklee College of Music in 2014 with a degree in Music Production and Engineering, I moved to Los Angeles to pursue my career. I worked under Johnny McDaid, then as a runner and assistant at Henson Recording Studios, now known as Chaplin. After that I was picked up by Terrace Martin in 2017 to engineer for him and worked all over LA, recording and mixing almost every day and night in world-class studios until the pandemic hit.

I then moved to Boston so I could be closer to family, and in 2021 started working in Studio Operations at Berklee College of Music as a Studio Manager. I'm still working with inspiring clients every day and love taking records where they feel like they need to go.

I've engineered/mixed two records that received Grammy Nominations; Dinner Party: Dessert and DRONES. Hopefully more to come soon, but that's not my only measure of success. Success to me is the ability to work on music that moves me.
